Business model

Free for patients. Commission on value created.

  • 1.Pharmacy commission — % on each prescription delivered via the platform
  • 2.Insurer commission / fee — per digitally validated coverage
  • 3.Premium consultation, then B2B2C employers & NGOs (year 2)

Collected via mobile money (Wave → Orange Money → MTN). Adoption first: patients never pay for basic access.
